I plan to remove most or all of my Glosa materials from my website over
the next few weeks. Assuming someone thinks they have value to the Glosa
community, I would like to transfer them to someone who might keep them
online. Please contact me off-list if you are interested in adopting any
of these pages. There are also two Russian files, written by someone
else, that could use a new home. 

As many of you know, my own goals have been diverging from those of the
Glosa community for a while. I have been frustrated with the imprecision
and complexity of the Glosa vocabulary, and with the inability to make
any progress towards cleaning up the syntax. The Glosa community seems
happy with Glosa in its present form, which I dislike, but respect.

What finally convinced me that I should distance myself from Glosa is
that one of the list members has recently treated several people very
rudely, without provocation. He has attacked "outsiders" who have
expressed an interest in Glosa, which reflects poorly on this community.
None of the core Glosa-pe have expressed any concern about this, and as
far as I know, no action was taken against this list member.

That's fine. It's your list and you are welcome to write what you want,
respond how you wish, and manage the list culture and membership as you
see fit. However, as a consequence, I no longer feel that this list is
compatible with my own values and goals.

It has been a fun couple years. I have learned a lot, and have enjoyed
many of our conversations. I wish each of you the best (including those
folks that I disagree with).

Vale,

Kevin

u-ci koncepa es fo importa, ka in plura natura
lingua il es plura elementa qi ne es nece pro 
korekti logi u frasi. U exempla mo in u-ci kampu

lingua ....in "ge-face lingua landa",

U lexi pro exempla :fat (patri)
                     fati (patri akuz.)
                     fata (de u patri gen.)
                     fate  (a patri dat.)
fat binom in dom  (patri es in domi.)
givob fate buki    (mi dona a patri u bibli.)
dom fata binon jönik  (domi de patri es kali.)
lofob fati  (mi filo u patri.).

elementa qi es plura kaso.
Hetero ge-face lingua, Esperanto ko-co pa uti

facili logi......

nece...pro salti pe debi nece akti kine..alo ne? Neo
